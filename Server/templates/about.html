About Smart-Bin

Revolutionizing waste management for Nashik's sacred sites

Our Mission

Smart-Bin was developed to address the unique waste management challenges faced by Nashik Municipal Corporation. As a major pilgrimage destination, Nashik welcomes millions of visitors each year, especially during festivals like Kumbh Mela.

Our mission is to ensure that these sacred spaces remain clean and welcoming for all visitors while helping the municipality operate more efficiently and sustainably.

Our Technology

IoT Sensors

Ultrasonic sensors measure waste levels in real-time and transmit data to our central server

Cloud Platform

Secure cloud infrastructure for reliable data storage and processing

Dashboard Interface

User-friendly monitoring system accessible to municipal staff

Analytics Engine

Predictive algorithms optimize collection schedules based on historical data

Our Impact on Nashik

Problem
